Concept

Acceptance test (TST)

Executable or specified acceptance behavior—often Gherkin-oriented—that tightens the link between UCs and stories.

In the spec tree

  • Id: TST-<NNN> — tests/TST-<NNN>-<slug>/TST-<NNN>.md (or equivalent per your tree).
  • Role: Express how you’ll know behavior is correct; pairs with test to story alignment.
  • CLI: uc to test, test to story — see the CLI reference.
