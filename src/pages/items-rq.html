Concept

Requirement theme (RQ)

Structured goals and constraints that organize use cases—your stable “what must be true” layer under change requests.

In the spec tree

  • Id: RQ-<NNN> — theme folder requirements/RQ-<NNN>-<slug>/ with RQ-<NNN>.md; UCs nest under the theme.
  • Role: Decomposes CR intent into testable themes and feeds UC authoring.
  • CLI: rq to uc, rq all — see the CLI reference.
