Story (STORY)

An implementable slice with clear acceptance and UC traceability—the unit of delivery your agents and engineers execute against.

In the spec tree

  • Id: STORY-<NNN> — under a SYS folder, e.g. technical/SYS-…/_STORY-<NNN>-<slug>/STORY-<NNN>.md (paths follow your CURSOR.md).
  • Role: Contract for implementation, aligned with horizontals where they apply.
  • CLI: story lifecycle and story close — see the CLI reference.
