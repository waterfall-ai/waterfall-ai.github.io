Why teams pick Waterfall

  • Living spec in Git — CRs, RQs, UCs, and stories stay versioned next to your product truth.
  • Guided workflows — CLI prompts mirror your team’s edges: CR → RQ → UC → story → test.
  • Horizontals — Cross-cutting models (API, data, stack) aggregate cleanly across stories.
  • Pluggable agents — Wire Cline, Cursor, or Claude Code; dry-run first, execute when you mean it.
