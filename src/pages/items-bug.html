Concept

Bug (BUG)

Defects and regressions tracked alongside stories under a subsystem, with ids that stay distinct from STORY work.

In the spec tree

  • Id: BUG-<NNN> — under technical/SYS-…/ in typed folders, parallel to story layout conventions in your spec.
  • Role: Capture failure mode, scope, and resolution without overloading STORY ids.
  • CLI: scaffolding and workflows as documented in the CLI reference.
