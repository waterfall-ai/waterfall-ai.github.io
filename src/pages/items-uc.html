Concept

Use case (UC)

Behavior from the actor’s perspective: flows, preconditions, and outcomes that stories and tests trace to.

In the spec tree

  • Id: UC-<NNN> — lives under an RQ theme, e.g. requirements/RQ-…/UC-<NNN>-<slug>/UC-<NNN>.md.
  • Role: Bridges requirements and implementation slices (stories) plus acceptance tests.
  • CLI: uc to story, uc to test, uc all — see the CLI reference.
