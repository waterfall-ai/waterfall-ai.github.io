Concept

Change request (CR)

The entry point for scoped change: intent, impact, and the thread that ties later RQs, UCs, and stories back to product decisions.

In the spec tree

  • Id: CR-<NNN> — one canonical CR-<NNN>.md per folder under changerequests/CR-<NNN>-<slug>/.
  • Role: Captures why the change exists and drives creation or revision of requirements and downstream work.
  • CLI: cr start, cr to rq, cr all, cr finish — see the CLI reference.
